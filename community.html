Parks & Recreation

Macon County offers beautiful parks and recreational facilities for residents and visitors to enjoy.

Parker Meadows Recreation Complex

Baseball fields, soccer fields, walking trails, and playground.
Address: 108 Ulco Drive, Franklin

Macon County Recreation Park

Community center, swimming pool, tennis courts, and picnic areas.
Address: 1288 Georgia Road, Franklin

Library Services

The Macon County Public Library is part of the Fontana Regional Library system and provides free library services to all residents.

Services Available

  • Book and media lending
  • Public computers and WiFi
  • Children's programs
  • Meeting rooms
  • Interlibrary loan

Library Hours

  • Monday - Thursday: 9 AM - 7 PM
  • Friday: 9 AM - 5 PM
  • Saturday: 9 AM - 4 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Phone: (828) 524-3600
