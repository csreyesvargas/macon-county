Gun Permits

Macon County has two types of gun permits for its citizens:

  • Permit to Purchase a Handgun
  • Concealed Handgun Permit

Contact the Sheriff's Office for application instructions.

Services

  • Basic life support (BLS)

Fire Marshal's Office

The Macon County Fire Marshal's Office, a division of the Macon County Emergency Services Office, performs fire inspections. Macon County has a fire prevention ordinance enforceable by civil and criminal penalties.

Fire Inspector: (828) 349-2068

Emergency Management

Macon County Emergency Management coordinates the county's preparedness, response, and recovery efforts for emergencies and disasters.

CodeRed Emergency Notifications

Be automatically notified in the event of an emergency situation in Macon County as well as other critical community alerts when you sign up for CodeRed.

Examples of notifications include:

  • Evacuation notices
  • Bio-terrorism alerts
  • Boil water notices
  • Missing child reports

911 Addressing

The 911 Addressing office assigns and maintains all addresses in Macon County to ensure emergency services can locate residents quickly.

Safe Newborn Surrender

North Carolina's Safe Surrender Law allows a parent to surrender a newborn baby (up to 7 days old) to a responsible adult at a hospital, health department, community health center, fire station, or EMS station, without fear of criminal prosecution.

Animal Services

Macon County Animal Services works to protect the health and safety of our residents, protect animals, promote their humane treatment, promote pet adoptions of healthy, nonaggressive animals by the general public, and promote responsible pet ownership.

Services

  • Animal control
  • Pet adoptions
  • Animal Control Ordinances enforcement
  • Bite investigations (with Environmental Health)

Contact Animal Services

Phone: (828) 349-2000
