Permits & Licenses

The Permits and Inspections department issues building permits and conducts inspections for construction projects in Macon County.

Types of Permits

  • Building Permits
  • Electrical Permits
  • Plumbing Permits
  • Mechanical Permits
  • Demolition Permits

How to Apply

Visit the Permits office at the County Administration Building or call (828) 349-2301 for more information.

Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Tax Information

The Macon County Tax Office is responsible for listing, appraising, and assessing all real and personal property within the county.

Property Taxes

  • Current tax rate: $0.3475 per $100 value
  • Bills mailed in July each year
  • Due date: January 5
  • Interest begins January 6

Payment Options

  • In person at the Tax Office
  • By mail
  • Online payment available
  • Drop box available 24/7

Contact: (828) 349-2100
