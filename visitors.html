Attractions

Macon County offers a variety of attractions for visitors to explore.

Downtown Franklin

Explore our charming downtown with unique shops, restaurants, and galleries. Don't miss the weekly farmers market on Saturdays.

Ruby & Gem Mining

Franklin is known as the "Gem Capital of the World." Visit one of our many gem mines to find rubies, sapphires, and other precious stones.

Festivals & Events

Enjoy annual events including the Macon County Fair, Scottish Heritage Festival, and the famous Pumpkin Fest.

Outdoor Recreation

Experience the natural beauty of the Blue Ridge Mountains with countless outdoor activities.

Waterfalls

  • Dry Falls
  • Cullasaja Falls
  • Bridal Veil Falls
  • Glen Falls

Hiking Trails

  • Appalachian Trail

  • Standing Indian Mountain

Where to Stay

Macon County offers a variety of lodging options to fit every budget and preference.

  • Hotels & Motels: Several chain and independent hotels in Franklin and Highlands
  • Bed & Breakfasts: Charming B&Bs throughout the county
  • Cabin Rentals: Mountain cabins with scenic views
  • Campgrounds: RV parks and tent camping in the Nantahala National Forest

Dining

From Southern comfort food to fine dining, Macon County has something for every taste.

  • Southern Cuisine: Traditional Appalachian and Southern cooking
  • Farm-to-Table: Restaurants featuring local ingredients
  • Casual Dining: Family-friendly restaurants and cafes
  • Fine Dining: Upscale options in Franklin and Highlands
