Economic Development

Macon County offers a business-friendly environment with a skilled workforce, affordable land, and excellent quality of life.

Why Choose Macon County?

  • Strategic location in Western North Carolina
  • Access to major highways (US 441, US 64, US 23)
  • Skilled and available workforce
  • Competitive operating costs
  • High quality of life
  • Business-friendly local government

Key Industries

  • Healthcare
  • Manufacturing
  • Tourism and Hospitality
  • Retail
  • Professional Services

Business Licenses

Information about obtaining business licenses and permits in Macon County.

County Privilege License

Most businesses operating in Macon County are required to obtain a privilege license. Contact the Tax Office for more information.

Common Permits Needed

  • Building Permit (for construction or renovation)
  • Sign Permit
  • Health Department Permit (for food service)
  • Zoning Compliance

Contact: Macon County Tax Office
Phone: (828) 349-2100

Zoning Information

The Macon County Planning Department administers zoning regulations to ensure orderly development.

Zoning Districts

  • R-1: Low Density Residential
  • R-2: Medium Density Residential
  • C-1: Commercial
  • I-1: Industrial
  • A-1: Agricultural

Zoning Services

  • Zoning verification letters
  • Special use permits
  • Variance applications
  • Rezoning requests

Contact: Planning Department
Phone: (828) 349-2300
