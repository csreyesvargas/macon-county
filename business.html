Economic Development

Macon County offers a business-friendly environment with a skilled workforce, affordable land, and excellent quality of life.

Why Choose Macon County?

  • Strategic location in Western North Carolina
  • Access to major highways (US 441, US 64, US 23)
  • Skilled and available workforce
  • Competitive operating costs
  • High quality of life
  • Business-friendly local government

Key Industries

  • Healthcare
  • Manufacturing
  • Tourism and Hospitality
  • Retail
  • Professional Services

Business Licenses

Information about obtaining business licenses and permits in Macon County.

County Privilege License

Most businesses operating in Macon County are required to obtain a privilege license. Contact the Tax Office for more information.

Common Permits Needed

  • Building Permit (for construction or renovation)
  • Sign Permit
  • Health Department Permit (for food service)
  • Zoning Compliance

Contact: Macon County Tax Office
Phone: (828) 349-2100

Planning & Development Ordinances

The County Planner administers the Sign, Telecommunication Tower, Subdivision, and Sexually Oriented Business ordinances. The Department also handles environmental services including Watershed Protection, Soil Erosion & Sedimentation Control, and Floodplain Management.

County Development Codes

  • Sign Control Ordinance
  • Subdivision Ordinance
  • Telecommunications Tower Ordinance
  • Watershed Protection
  • Soil Erosion & Sedimentation Control
  • Floodplain Management
  • High Impact Land Use

NC State Building Codes Enforced

  • NC Building Code
  • NC Electrical Code
  • NC Mechanical Code
  • NC Plumbing Code
  • NC Fire Prevention Code
